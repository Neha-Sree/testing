## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: PNG (with transparent background if possible)
- **Size**: 200x200 pixels (square)
- **Style**: Clean, professional, medical/educational
- **Background**: White or transparent
- **Quality**: High resolution, clear and recognizable

## Usage:
These images will be displayed in the baby size hero banner to help mothers visualize their baby's growth throughout pregnancy. Each image corresponds to a specific week of pregnancy and shows the approximate size of the baby.

## After Uploading:
1. Make sure all images are in the `assets/images/` folder
2. Update `pubspec.yaml` to include the assets section
3. The hero banner will automatically display the correct image based on pregnancy week
